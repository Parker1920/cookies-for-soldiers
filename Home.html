Our Mission

Sending Christmas cheer to our Troops during the Holiday Season in the form of HOmemade Cookies and Candies as a reminder of the love, prayers and support to them while they are providing Freedom to us so we can enjoy family time and create those memories they have of home during the Holiday Season.
