Who We Are

In 2012 I was a little 10 year old with a big imagination. I went to Katyes Helping Hands annual motercycle ride. On my way home my grandmother, Sue Swartz, and I camp up with a plan to help deployed military troops. Since Christmas was coming up soon, we decided to send cookies to troops so they can have a taste of home while defending our country. That year we started our collection, and we collected 107 dozen. Over the past 5 years we have received many gifts from from the deployed troops, including flags, troops coins and many many cards.
